Demo scaffold for card interaction. Includes a two-sided board with 3 card slots plus a dedicated deck slot per side, a hand row, hold-to-zoom preview, and drag-to-snap placement.

Interaction

Hold your board/hand cards for an animated close-up, or drag and release to snap them into one of your three side card slots. Deck slots are reserved for future draw animations and networked game flow.
